Longhorn Racing Solar


  • Array/MPPT Lead
  • The goal for LHR Solar is to build a car powered by solar panels to be raced around a circiut
  • As Array Lead, I am responisble with all things to do with power generation and power efficieny
  • I spend most of my time researching, implmenting, and refining various Maximum Power Point Tracking (MPPT) algorithms to maximize efficiency in the solar array
  • Algorithms are tested and optimized using a team-made simulator that models the behavior of solar array modules given temperature and irradiance conditions
  • We have done significant work in the research of MPPT algorithms under partial shading conditions with multiple local maxima
  • I also look over the manufacturing PCBs and hardware for the arrays
  • Click here for the github to the array team.
